Timeline
  • The timeline contains a list of all your confirmed scheduled appointments
  • Confirmed scheduled appointments contain the following information:
    • Medical specialist type (ie dentist, eye doctor, family doctor)
    • Doctor name (ie Dr. Help)
    • Location (ie 83 GoodHealth St.)
    • Date (ie Jan 14, 2022)
    • Time (ie 4:30 PM)
  • You can create a new appointment by pressing the Create New Appointment button, filling in the relevant information into the draft appointment dialog, and then saving your work
  • You can edit an appointment by pressing the button with the pencil and paper icon; the existing appointment information will appear there at first but will be editable, except for the given medical specialist type
  • You can delete an appointment by pressing the button with the garbage icon
  • You can use the scrollbar to view appointments that are not in the current view
  • Appointments will expire if the date they occur has passed
  • Any changes made to the timeline (ie deleting, editing, creating) will be reflected accordingly by the timeline
Suggestions
  • Suggestions contains a list of suggested appointment information
  • Suggested appointments are essentially recommendations for possible future scheduled appointments based on your existing appointments
  • ex. A dentist appointment will have a recommended booking date 180 days (about 6 months) after the appointment it is based on
  • Suggested appointments will display similar information to a confirmed scheduled appointment, with the addition of the suggested date to book the future appointment with your doctor
  • Suggested dates for booking will be the same as the day the confirmed scheduled appointment the suggested appointment is based on occurs (will be labeled as "Now" if the day of occurrence is today)
  • Any changes made to the timeline will be reflected accordingly by the suggestions
